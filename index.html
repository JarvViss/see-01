OUR STORY

From a vision to India's first anonymous job platform

Hey! We're the founders of Hire22. Our journey began in 2022 when we identified a critical gap in the recruitment industry. As professionals ourselves, we experienced firsthand how traditional hiring processes often exposed candidates' identities too early, leading to missed opportunities and privacy concerns.

We saw talented professionals hesitating to explore new opportunities because they feared their current employers would find out. Companies were missing out on exceptional candidates who couldn't risk their privacy. This inspired us to create a revolutionary solution.

With backgrounds in technology and recruitment, we built Hire22 as India's first anonymous job platform. Our innovative approach allows professionals to showcase their skills and experience while maintaining complete control over their identity until they're ready to reveal it.

Today, Hire22 has connected over 50,000 anonymous profiles with leading employers across 6 countries, reducing average connection time to just 22 hours. We're proud to be transforming how mid and senior professionals find their next big role—confidentially, efficiently, and on their own terms.
